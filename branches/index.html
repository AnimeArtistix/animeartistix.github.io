Information about branches and how they work

What are branches and how do they work? A branch in this group is a part of AnimeArtistix in which someone can own a small part of this group for a topic or interest. Branches will have their own page.
If the branches have information in them, then the owner of that branch must check if the information is right using outside sources, and/or experience.
Branches will have their own page, and if the page has information, then there will be links at the bottom of the page that show citations/ websites that you used for the info.

What are sub-branches and how they work: Sub-branches work as a smaller branch that exists inside a branch. For example, there could be a sub-branch about a video game like Minecraft or Roblox inside of the gaming branch. A sub-branch can be found inside of the branch page.

Note: Branches that are crossed out were made by people that used to be in the group because they were either kicked out or they left on their own.
